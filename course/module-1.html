THE CORE PRINCIPLE

THE BIG IDEA

The first 72 hours, and the first 90 days, are when members decide whether your membership becomes a habit or gets forgotten. Your member's motivation is highest right now and will never be this high again.

THREE CRITICAL WINDOWS
Buyer's Remorse Window
Immediately after purchase, doubt creeps in. "Did I make the right choice? Can I afford this? What will my spouse say?" You need to address this before they do.
Motivation Decay
Motivation peaks at signup and declines rapidly. If you don't capture that energy immediately, you lose it forever. Every day you wait is a day closer to cancel.
The Success Path Principle
Overwhelm kills retention. Members need a clear, guided journey. Not a library dump. If they can't see the path, they'll make their own exit.

THE GOAL OF ACTIVATION

Accomplish these six things in your onboarding and you've done your job:

  • Validation: Remind them they just made the right choice
  • Resell the Vision: Why they bought this. Because they've already forgotten
  • Counter Objections: Preempt self-doubt, spousal objections, buyer's remorse
  • Clarify the Path: Tell them exactly what happens next. Eliminate confusion
  • Quick Win: Get them a tangible result as fast as humanly possible
  • Connection Point: Get them into the community immediately

THE SCIENCE

BJ FOGG'S BEHAVIOR MODEL

For any behavior to occur, three elements must converge simultaneously: Motivation (desire to do it), Ability (ease of doing it), and Trigger (prompt to do it). At signup, all three naturally align. Your job is to act before they stop.

M
Motivation: Desire to do it
A
Ability: Ease of doing it
T
Trigger: Prompt to do it
"Doing must be easier than thinking. If a new member has to think about what to do next, you've already lost them."

TIME TO FIRST VALUE

Your north star metric for Activation.

Definition: The time between signup and the member achieving their first tangible result. The closer to zero, the better.

  • Members who hit a quick win in the first week stay 3x longer
  • Every day you delay first value, churn risk increases by ~5%
  • Trust is built through delivered promises, not content consumed
INDUSTRY BENCHMARKS
Under 7 days: Great
You're giving members a win before doubt has time to set in.
7-14 days: Good
Still within the window. Push it earlier if you can.
15-30 days: Risky
Motivation is fading. Churn risk rising.
30+ days: Danger Zone
You've lost them mentally. The cancel is just paperwork at this point.

THE SIX STRATEGIES

01
THE WELCOME VIDEO

What it is: A welcome video that actively combats buyer's remorse. Not just "thanks for joining." The first thing a member experiences after purchase sets the tone for everything.

THE WELCOME VIDEO SCRIPT FRAMEWORK
0:00 to 0:30: Congratulate & Validate
"You just made an incredible decision. You invested [money] and committed to [transformation]. You made the right call. Here's why..."
0:30 to 1:30: Resell the Benefits
"You joined because [specific pain point]. Over the next [timeframe], you're going to [transformation]. Here's exactly what that looks like..."
1:30 to 2:30: Preempt Objections
"In the next 24 hours you might have a little voice saying 'Can I really do this?' Here's what to tell that voice: You can. I've seen people just like you succeed."
2:30 to 3:00: Clarify Next Steps
"In the next 5 minutes, you're going to [first action]. Takes under [time] and will give you [quick win]."
3:00 to 3:30: First Action
"Right now, before you do anything else, click the button below and [complete first action]. See you on the other side."
02
FRICTIONLESS FIRST ACTION

Completable in under 10 minutes. Impossible to mess up. Delivers a tangible result. Design for the guaranteed win. Completion builds momentum. Failure builds doubt.

FITNESS MEMBERSHIP
Action: "Complete your body composition benchmark"
Result: They have a baseline. They've proven they can track progress.
BUSINESS COACHING
Action: "Calculate your current monthly recurring revenue"
Result: They know their starting point. They can measure growth.
CREATIVE COURSE
Action: "Create your first 30-second video using this template"
Result: They've proven they can create. They have something to share.
03
THE SUCCESS PATH

A clear, linear roadmap. The full transformation must be visible from Day 1. "I'll never catch up" and "I don't know where to start" are silent killers. Both are solved by a visible path.

90-DAY SUCCESS PATH STRUCTURE
Week 1: Foundation
Day 1: Benchmark/first action · Day 3: Core concept #1 · Day 5: First implementation · Day 7: Community introduction
Weeks 2 to 4: Momentum
Week 2: Core concept #2 + action · Week 3: Core concept #3 + action · Week 4: First milestone review
Month 2 to 3: Transformation
Month 2: Intermediate tactics · Month 3: First major result
Month 4 to 6: Mastery
Advanced strategies, case studies, community leadership
04
COMMUNITY ONBOARDING

Get members into the community and introducing themselves within 72 hours. Community creates relational switching costs. Members stay because of connections, not just content.

60%
Higher 90-day retention for members who engage in community during Week 1
The faster you create a connection, the harder it becomes to leave. Don't wait for them to find each other. Introduce them.
INTRODUCTION TEMPLATE TO GIVE NEW MEMBERS
"Hi, I'm [name], and I joined because [specific pain/goal]. One thing I'm excited to learn is [specific outcome]. One thing I'm nervous about is [vulnerability]. Looking forward to connecting with you all!"
05
HIGH-TOUCH AUTOMATION

Automation that feels human. Members want to feel special. You can't personally call 100 signups. Smart automation bridges the gap without the labor.

RINGLESS VOICEMAIL: SLYBROADCAST / S2VM (AU)
"Hey [Name], it's [Your Name] from [Membership]. I just saw you joined and wanted to personally welcome you. Check your email for your welcome video. Looking forward to seeing your progress."

Why it works: Feels personal. Takes no real-time. Pattern interrupt. Unexpected delight. Costs almost nothing.
06
RECOUP YOUR INVESTMENT

Create a first module that shows members how to make their money back immediately. Once they've recouped the investment, every future month feels like pure value, not cost.

THE PSYCHOLOGY

Subscription math feels completely different when the membership is "free." A business coaching membership that helped you land one client doesn't feel like a $497/month expense anymore. It feels like leverage.

THE ETHICS CHECK

❌ DON'T DO THIS
Weaponize buyer's remorse with fear or shame
Create false urgency: "You MUST complete this in 24 hours or fail!"
Dump and ghost: 100 modules and "good luck"
Over-promise: "You'll be a millionaire in 30 days!"
✓ DO THIS INSTEAD
Validate their decision and counter genuine objections
Show them the path and help them start with confidence
Provide a clear success path with ongoing support
Set realistic expectations with achievable quick wins
"If my best friend joined my membership, would I be proud of this onboarding experience?"
